Affordable Prosthetics

With the discovery that myo-electric prosthetic hands cost between 20,000-200,000 USD and are not insured by health care in Canada. NeurobioNX was created. A startup that aim at building a myo-electric prosthetic with accuracy and functionality in mind while keeping the cost to below 5,000 CAD. Unfortunately, the startup had to be stopped during COVID times. However, the efforts are still being pushed forward to create free, open-source myoelectric hands that can be 3D-printed and built anywhere in the world.
